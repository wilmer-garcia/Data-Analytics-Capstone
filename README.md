# Data-Analytics-Capstone
In this project, we developed an analysis for Ada Analytics to transform and evaluate client segmentation, revenue risks, pricing impacts, and key operational metrics. The goal was to build a dashboard that supports account managers and strengthens strategic decision‑making.
Role: I analyzed client and operational data, identified key performance patterns, and contributed recommendations based on the insights generated from the dashboard. I also supported the interpretation of segmentation logic, revenue risk indicators, and pricing simulations to guide strategic decisions.
Key Skills: 
- Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query, DAX logic)
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Segmentation & ROI Analysis
- Scenario Modeling (What‑If Simulation)
- Dashboard Design & UX Structuring (Power BI)
- KPI Development & Insight Communication
- Business Problem Framing & Recommendation Building

Project Highlights:
- Built a client segmentation model that grouped customers into four ROI‑based clusters (CGC, EFG, PCG, MCG) to clarify revenue contribution and strategic priorities.
- Developed a What‑If Pricing Simulation to model revenue impact from rate adjustments in real time.
- Implemented Attendance Logic to automatically flag no‑shows and quantify opportunity cost.
- Created Aging Visibility to highlight over $9K in overdue invoices, supporting targeted collection efforts.
- Designed a dual‑audience dashboard tailored for account managers (operational insights) and administrators (financial and strategic insights).

How to Navigate the Repo
- Dashboard: Power BI files, visuals, and DAX measures used in the final dashboard.
- Analysis: Data exploration notes, segmentation logic, and KPI definitions.
- Documentation: Project summary, methodology, and recommendations.
- Data: Sample or anonymized datasets used for demonstration (no real client data).

Privacy & Client: All data used in this repository has been anonymized, down‑sampled, or replaced with synthetic values to protect client confidentiality. No identifiable or sensitive information is included.
